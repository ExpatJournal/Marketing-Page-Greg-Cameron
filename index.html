Pin locations

Turn on the GPS tracker, drop a pin, and snap a pic. Your location will automatically be tied to your photo! When you upload your photos a map pin will be added to your footprints map!

Once you're ready to write your travel blog, your photos will already be organized and waiting for you! Just drag and drop photos from your album to your blogg!
